PangPond Bot Privacy Policy
Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains what information PangPond Bot collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information We Collect
PangPond Bot may collect and store:
* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Card inventories
* Cooldowns and reminders
* Game progress and achievements
* Bot usage data necessary for functionality
  
PangPond Bot does not collect:
* Passwords
* Email addresses
* Payment information
* Private Discord messages

3. How We Use Information
Collected information is used solely to:
* Operate bot features
* Save inventories and collections
* Manage cooldowns and reminders
* Provide game functionality
* Improve bot performance

4. Data Sharing
PangPond Bot does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties except when required by law.

5. Data Storage
Data is stored in a secure database and retained only as long as necessary to provide bot services.

6. User Rights
Users may request the deletion of their game data by contacting the PangPond development team through the official support server.

7. Security
Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of PangPond Bot constitutes acceptance of any updates.

9. Contact
For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data requests, contact the PangPond development team through the official support server.
